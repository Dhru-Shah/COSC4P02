Experience Niagara's history digitally

The Niagara Historical Society was founded in 1895 with the aim of promoting an appreciation for Niagara-on-the-Lake's rich heritage. Within a year, the Society had amassed a considerable collection of artifacts, which led to the opening of a museum in the local Courthouse. In 1907, under the guidance of the Society's President, Janet Carnochan, they inaugurated Memorial Hall, Ontario's first purpose-built museum.
